Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Take Note Pro ("the App") is a timecode logging application for film and television production professionals. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following data to provide and improve the App:

  • Account information: Email address and authentication credentials when you create an account (via email, Apple Sign In, or Google Sign In).
  • Session data: Timecode notes, session metadata, mic lists, and other content you create within the App. This data is stored in Google Firestore to enable cloud synchronisation across your devices.
  • Uploaded documents: Call sheets, scripts, and other files you upload are stored locally on your device and may be synced via Firestore.
  • Subscription information: Payment and subscription status managed through Apple In-App Purchases, Stripe, or RevenueCat. We do not store your payment card details.
  • Device information: Basic device and platform information to ensure the App functions correctly.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to:

  • Provide and operate the App's features
  • Synchronise your sessions and notes across devices
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Improve the App's performance and reliability

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase services (Authentication and Firestore), which provide industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Subscription data is processed securely through Apple, Stripe, and RevenueCat.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data may be processed by the following services solely to operate the App:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication and cloud data storage
  • Apple: In-App Purchase processing
  • Stripe: Web payment processing
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management across platforms

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data (sessions, notes, account information) is permanently removed from our servers.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: All your data is visible within the App.
  • Export your data: You can export your sessions and notes in CSV, EDL, FCPXML, and other formats.
  • Delete your data: You can delete individual sessions within the App, or delete your entire account and all associated data through the App's account settings.
  • Withdraw consent: You can stop using the App and delete your account at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information.

9. Cookies and Tracking

The App does not use cookies or third-party tracking technologies for advertising purposes. Firebase may use local storage for authentication tokens and session management.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the App or via email. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us at joel@slowwaveaudio.tv.
